Car Cleaning

The what!

Novowash specializes in interior cleaning of cars. We know that you love your car and we at Novowash have carefully chosen technologies to cater to your needs.

The why!

Car interior detailing is often neglected, as local-washermen lack the expertise and technologies to cater to the demands of interior cleaning. Also, with our busy schedule, it is practically diffucult to visit and leave our cars back at car washing stations.

We at Novowash have given a thorough research and brought to the market, services which add value to poeple's lives and in this regards for the very first time have introduced the concept of "monthly" car interior cleaning services. This is primarily so that our customers can drive in clean interiors not just once but all year round.

Market Study

As per our market-research, the following are the major areas which need regular cleaning with respect to car interiors:

• Spotting and staining of seats
• Dirtying for car carpets due to dust and food spill-overs
• Darkening for car roof-tops due to carbon deposition
• Dusty and pollution laden AC vents
• Carbon and dust accumulation on car engine
• Bad aroma inside of cars
• Often dusty and unclean car boots

If as a regular commuter, you too feel face the similar issues, please drop in a request to Novowash and try our services.

We at Novowash consider that a car also needs to be cleaned on the exteriors along with interiors. In-order to give our customers a total makeover we offer 'free water-less exterior cleaning' along with all our interior cleaning services. And for the ulitmate total car cleaning, we have introduced high pressure steam cleaning!
