Our Vision

"To bring ease and convenience in car and home interior care using technology."

What is Novowash?

We are India's first door-step monthly car interior cleaning providers, who strive to deliver elite customer experience in interior maintainance. We built technology driven operations that leverage on steam technologies to not just clean but sanitize your interiors. We build services that solve customer problems in interior cleaning and mobile-friendly platforms for service requests.

Who are we?

We surround ourselves with smart people who strive to build products which cater to the actual needs of a consumer. We believe in technology to solve problems. We built a team which consists primarily of technology driven people to achieve our vision.
