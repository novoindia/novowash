  • 02

    How to identify the car variants ( Sedan vs SUV vs Hatch-back)?

    A Sedan comprises of 4 doors or less and 2 rows of seats or less with extended boot space. A SUV consists of a 3rd row of seats and/or a third door on the back of the vehicle. A Hatch-back consists of 4 doors or less and 2 rows of seats or less and without extended boot space.
